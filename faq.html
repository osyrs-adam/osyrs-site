Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about how Osyrs works today - and where it’s heading next.

What is Osyrs?
Osyrs is an AI-powered financial operating system that helps you understand, optimise and automate your financial life using real-time intelligence and machine learning.
Is Osyrs a bank?
Not yet. Osyrs launches as a smart intelligence layer using secure open banking. Over time, it will evolve into a full banking experience with accounts, intelligent cards and autonomous money movement.
How does Osyrs connect to my bank?
Osyrs uses licensed Open Banking providers to securely read your transactions. You stay in full control - Osyrs never touches or holds your money.
How does Osyrs use my data?
Your data is encrypted, never sold, and only used to power your personal financial model. You control what’s connected and can disconnect at any time.
What is Osyrs Plus?
Osyrs Plus unlocks deeper insights, spending intelligence, predictive modelling, and advanced automation features.
What is Osyrs Pro?
Osyrs Pro will offer curated lifestyle perks, intelligent event planning, deeper automation, agentic investment capabilities and premium experiences embedded directly within the OS.
When will Osyrs launch?
Osyrs is currently in active development. Join the waitlist to get early access and updates as we ship new phases.
Which countries will Osyrs support first?
Osyrs will launch first in the UAE and UK, with global expansion planned.
Will Osyrs work with my existing bank?
Yes. Osyrs connects directly to your existing accounts through secure Open Banking.
What devices will Osyrs support?
Osyrs will launch on iOS and Android, with a web dashboard to follow.
