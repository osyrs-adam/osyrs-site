Conversational Automation

Tell Osyrs your intention - “I want £500 left this month.” It analyses your world, adjusts your spending and rules and makes it happen. One request. Zero manual effort.

02

Universal Aggregation

Osyrs connects securely to your bank accounts using licensed open-banking providers. Your transactions are analysed in real time to power the spending intelligence, predictions and automation that make Osyrs feel truly autonomous - without ever touching your money.

03

Dynamic Rebalancing

Osyrs continuously adjusts your allocations as life shifts - rent goes out, bills fluctuate or you spend more in certain categories. It rebalances your plan automatically, keeping your month stable without you lifting a finger.
